Tears of the Kingdom

Breath of the Wild's Sequel

I've always enjoyed Zelda games but Breath of the Wild is what made me truly love the franchise.

The game is absolutely beautiful and there is seemingly endless amounts of things to do.

So when the sequel, Tears of the Kingdom, was announced, I knew I had to get it on release day.

Tears of The Kingdom

This game is just as gorgeous as Breath of the Wild, but with twice the amount of things to do.

The building and fusing mechanic introduces a new layer of creativity. I'm also really enjoying the new outfits offered in this game.

As of now I am nowhere close to beating the game and I'm perfectly fine with that. Slowly discovering everything the game has to offer makes each playing session fun.
