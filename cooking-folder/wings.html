Chili Lime Chicken Wings

Ingredients

  • 2 pound chicken wings
  • 1 tsp garlic salt
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 tsp papripka powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch

Put chicken wings in a bowl. Add spices and mix well with wings. Optional: Marinate wings in fridge.

Airfry wings for 30-32 minutes at 375 degrees farenheit, flipping the wings in the fryer halfway through.

Wings are good to consume now but I would recommend creating a sauce to complement the wings.

Sauce recipe

  • 1 lime
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 2 tbsp chili oil
  • 1 tsp butter

I don't usually have chili oil on hand but it can be made by heating up some oil, adding roughly chopped dried chili peppers, a minced garlic clove, and some paprika powder.

In a sauce pan, melt the butter. Add chili oil and squeeze limes in. Stir.

Add the honey as well as lime zest. Mix with wings and enjoy.

These chili lime wings are insanely good and very easy to make. The wings are a perfect blend of spicy and tangy, with a hint of sweetness from the honey.
